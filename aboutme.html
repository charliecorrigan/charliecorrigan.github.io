Hello, there! I'm Charlie Corrigan and it's a pleasure to meet you.


I live, program, and paint in Denver, Colorado. I graduated from the University of Colorado at Denver with a BA in English Literary Studies. I've spent most of my time since then working for the library, travelling, painting, reading, and gardening. Just recently, I embarked on a new adventure and am currently studying back-end engineering at Turing School of Software & Design.


Please enjoy your visit and feel welcome to follow me on Twitter or connect with me on LinkedIn or GitHub.
